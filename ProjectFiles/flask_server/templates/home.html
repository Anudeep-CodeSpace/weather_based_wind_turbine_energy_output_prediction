Why it matters

The case for
wind energy

Zero Emissions

Wind turbines generate electricity with no direct CO₂ or greenhouse gas emissions during operation, making them one of the cleanest energy sources available at industrial scale.

Infinite Fuel Source

Wind is inexhaustible and available everywhere on the planet. Unlike fossil fuels subject to depletion and geopolitical risk, the wind will keep blowing for billions of years.

Energy Independence

Distributed wind generation reduces reliance on centralized fossil fuel infrastructure and cross-border energy imports, enhancing national and regional energy security.

Falling Costs

Wind LCOE has dropped over 70% in the last decade. Predictive analytics like this tool further cut operational costs by optimizing maintenance scheduling and grid dispatch decisions.
